# Transition Signals

Brainstorming ends only on explicit transition intent.

## Valid Transition Signals

- "Stop brainstorming."
- "Pass this to dev-investigate."
- "Turn this into a spec."
- "Start implementation."

Equivalent wording in the user's language counts when the intent is clear.

## Not Enough On Their Own

- "This is getting good."
- "What should we do next?"
- "I think we are close."

When the user sounds ready to move forward but does not name the next step, ask one short clarification question and remain in brainstorming mode.
